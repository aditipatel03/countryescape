Description

Golden Crest Manor Villa is a luxurious 3 BHK property with a private swimming pool, is situated in the picturesque town of Lonavala. The villa, a masterpiece of architectural design, features spacious living areas adorned with tasteful decor and high-end furnishings. Each of the four bedrooms exudes refinement, ensuring guests enjoy privacy and comfort. Property Layout: Ground plus 1 Bedrooms: - This Villa boasts 3 bedrooms, situated on the First Floor. - Every bedroom is equipped with an air conditioner. Fresh linen and pillows are provided in all rooms. Wardrobes are available. One of the bathrooms is equipped with a luxurious bathtub All bathrooms are modern and have geysers for hot water supply. Living Room: - The living room has sofa seater for 9 people. - A dining table for 4 people is also available within the living room. Bathrooms: - All bedrooms have attached bathrooms equipped with basic toiletries. - A common bathroom is available in the living room. Kitchen & Meals: - Heavy cooking is prohibited. - The kitchen is semi-equipped with essential appliances, including a refrigerator, microwave and water purifier. - Cutlery and crockery are readily available to use for up to 12 people. Outdoor Area: - The villa has a huge lawn. - A private indoor pool is available from 2pm till 10pm, the pool size is (18x10x4.5ft approx.). - The lawn has seating arrangement for 10-12 people Adult supervision is required for children at all times in the pool area.
